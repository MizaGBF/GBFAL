FAQ

• What is this?

This page catalogs assets for the mobile game Granblue Fantasy.
Users often find themselves tinkering with URLs or utilizing Google Chrome Dev Tools when attempting to retrieve specific art pieces.
The purpose of this page is to address this issue by offering a straightforward method for accessing assets, including those that may be challenging to locate through other means.
In contrast to comparable projects, this one refrains from hosting any content. This approach aims to maintain a streamlined, rapid, and minimalist approach.
Consequently, it's important to acknowledge that assets could potentially become inaccessible or undergo modifications at the discretion of Cygames.
Keep in mind: Granblue Fantasy content and materials are trademarks and copyrights of Cygames, Inc. or its licensors. All rights reserved.

• When does it get updated?

Updates to this page require manual interventions.
As such, you can expect them to occur between 15:00 and 08:00 JST.
It means that updates won't occur at the time of end-of-month Premium Galas, for example. Please be patient or do a manual search to get partial results.
In the event that you observe an update to the GitHub repository but no corresponding changes on this page, try a forced refresh through CTRL+F5 or clearing your cache.

• What does "this element isn't indexed" mean?

It denotes that the specific content you are attempting to access hasn't been fully cataloged.
It can occur when conducting manual searches or when seeking main character job results.
In essence, this message signifies that the page has yet to be updated for the element you are currently viewing.
You can expect this message to disappear after the next upate.
Bring it to my attention should it persist.

• I found a missing asset!

Upon discovering a missing asset or identifying an error, please send me the asset's url and the ID of the element.
You can find ways to contact me on the bottom of my home page.

• I found a bug!

If you noticed a problem or bug on this page, you can open an issue in the GitHub repository or contact me directly.
Try to explain clearly what the problem is and provide screenshots, if possible.

• Can you add [...] ?

The following won't be added, either for technical reasons, because it wouldn't be very useful or because it would be beyond the scope of this project:
Fate Episode/Event/Boss voice lines, Musics, Skill/Buff/Items Icons.
It might change in the future.

• Unreleased asset policy

Concerning unreleased assets, there are instances where Cygames inadvertently makes assets from upcoming content accessible to the general public.
In such occurrences, to prevent any potential complications, you can expect me to promptly delist these assets, if it's apparent that they were intended to remain concealed and if Cygames hasn't already addressed the situation.

• Can I use this project?

The project is open source, under the MIT License.
In the advent that I quit or disappear, you can do whatever you want, within the license scopes, with this project.

• How can I support you?

You can leave a star on the GitHub repository.
