Last update:
  • A valid ID looks like 3040000000.
  • You can find most of them on the wiki,
    under the extra tab.
  • You can also search characters, summons or weapons
    by Tags.
  • For Enemies, add 'e' before the ID. Example:
    e6205703 for School Belial.
  • For Classes, simply input the main class ID:
    450301 for Relic Buster.
  • Click on images to access their highest quality.
