Known Issues

  • The Character list is shuffled at the start
  • Toggling off a category will remove all corresponding characters from the pool
  • "First Version" is the first release of a character, "Alternate Versions" are the subsequent releases of a character
  • "Multi Units" are all characters with "and" in their names
  • "Multi Units" might count as multiple characters if there are multiple races/genders
  • Newer characters might take a few days before being added to the list
