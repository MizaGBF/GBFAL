How-To

  • Click a Character or Summon to add it to the list.
  • or Hold Shift before adding a Character to add it to the Moon Column.
  • Click on an Element to Remove it from the list.
  • Or Drag Elements to add, remove or reorder them.
  • On PC, Ctrl+Click and Drag on an added element to copy it.
  • On Mobile, start sliding horizontally on a bottom element before dragging it to the top. Otherwise you'll scroll the section.
  • or Hold Shift and click on an Element to toggle its Sparked state.
